What is MacroMan?

MacroMan is a tool that makes tracking your macronutrients simple.

How do you use it?

Start by entering your macronutrients in grams. A search bar will drop down along with your chart of calories and macronutrients. Search for the food you want, when you find it, add it and watch your calories change!
